## Anti-Pitches (What NOT to say)

❌ "It's like Slack for AI"
- Too vague, doesn't explain visual aspect

❌ "Dashboard for monitoring agents"
- Boring, doesn't convey fun/gamification

❌ "Web-based agent management system"
- Corporate speak, kills excitement

❌ "Visualization tool for AI workflows"
- Too abstract, people won't get it

✅ **DO say:** "Pixel art NPCs for your AI agents"
- Concrete, visual, intriguing
