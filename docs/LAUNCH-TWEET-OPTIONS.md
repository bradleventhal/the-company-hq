## Pro Tips

**1. Attach the demo GIF:**
- File: `public/openclawfice-demo.gif`
- Shows live office in action
- Twitter favors media tweets

**2. Pin the tweet:**
- After posting, click "..." → "Pin to profile"
- Keeps it visible for 24-48 hours

**3. Respond to every reply:**
- Within 5 minutes if possible
- Engagement = algorithm boost
- Build community early

**4. Quote tweet yourself 2-4 hours later:**
- "Already at 50+ stars! 🚀"
- "Demo is getting crushed (in a good way)"
- Share user feedback screenshots
