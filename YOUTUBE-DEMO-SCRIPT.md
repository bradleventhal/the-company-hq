## 📝 SCRIPT NOTES

### Tone & Delivery
- **Casual, conversational** - not corporate
- **Enthusiasm without hype** - show, don't tell
- **Quick pace** - keep it moving, no dead air
- **Retro gaming references** - The Sims, RPGs, pixel art vibes

### Visual Tips
- **Show, don't narrate** - let the UI speak for itself
- **Smooth transitions** - no jarring cuts
- **Highlight interactions** - cursor movements, clicks, animations
- **Music** - retro chiptune background, low volume

### Key Selling Points to Emphasize
1. **Visual vs invisible** - biggest pain point solved
2. **Fun factor** - gamification makes monitoring enjoyable  
3. **Zero config** - auto-discovery, works out of the box
4. **Real-time updates** - live status, not static dashboards
5. **Multi-agent coordination** - water cooler chat = actual teamwork

### Optional B-Roll Ideas
- Split-screen: terminal chaos vs OpenClawfice calm
- Time-lapse: agents moving between rooms over 30 mins
- Close-up: NPC expressions changing based on mood
- Quest completion: XP notification popping up
- Meeting room: 3 agents collaborating in real-time
