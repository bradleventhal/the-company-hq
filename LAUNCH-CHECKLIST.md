## 💡 Quick Tips

### Do This
- ✅ Lead every post with the demo link
- ✅ Respond to feedback within 24h
- ✅ Ship small updates weekly
- ✅ Thank contributors publicly
- ✅ Ask for GitHub stars (it's okay!)

### Don't Do This
- ❌ Argue with critics — learn instead
- ❌ Let repo go stale (commit weekly)
- ❌ Ignore early users for growth
- ❌ Over-promise features
- ❌ Add paywall too early
